Introduction

It is very likely that you have stumbled upon the saying “Garbage in, Garbage out” when you read articles or books about machine learning. That's way data preprocessing is a fundamental step in the machine learning lifecycle, significantly impacting model performance, efficiency, and even fairness.

In this three-part series, we'll delve into the world of data preprocessing using scikit-learn pipelines. Part 1 will explore the 'why' and 'how' of preprocessing, focusing on standard transformers and their limitations. Part 2 will explore more advanced techniques for handling categorical data and building custom transformers, while part 3 will introduce scikit-learn's ColumnTransformer, which is suitable for applying transformations to heterogeneous datasets (datasets with different data types).

Why do we even want to preprocess out data?

Clean and properly scaled data is critical to successfully for training machine learning models. While not every machine learning model needs data that lives in the same scale, it's not a disadvantage for these models if they do so.
