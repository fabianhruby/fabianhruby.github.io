Introduction

Building on our understanding of data preprocessing with Scikit-learn Pipeline from the first part of this series, let's explore how to transform categorical data. This part goes beyond basic techniques, introducing custom transformers for tailored data cleaning.

Our introductory example was very simple in this respect, as we only had to deal with one missing numerical value. With categorical variables, things look a little different – especially if we not only want to replace missing values, but also want to clean up messy data, for example.

In this part of the series, we want to look at transforming categorical data with standard techniques like one-hot encoding but also introducing custom transformers, which allow us to to define custom preprocessing functions to address specific needs based on the available data.
